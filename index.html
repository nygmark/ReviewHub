About

     The ReviewHub is a dedicated interface that helps users efficiently review and track their learning materials. It is designed to consolidate all relevant study content into one accessible page, making it easy to stay organized and focused.

     With features that support active recall, spaced repetition, and progress tracking, this program enables users to reinforcentheir learning and make steady progress. Whether you are preparing for an exam or just looking to learn new skills. The ReviewHub provides streamlined way to manage your study materials and achieve your goals.

How to Use?

     Below, you will find four interactive cards designed to enhance your learning experience. These cards offer a range tools to help you to study, plan, and stay focused.

     The four cards include Flashcards, Quiz, Planner, and Pomodoro. The Flashcards card is perfect for reviewing key terms and concepts, while the Quiz card let's you test your knowledge with interactive questions. The Planner card helps you to stay organized and on track, and the Pomodoro card is a productivity tool that uses timed intervals to boost your focus and concentration.
